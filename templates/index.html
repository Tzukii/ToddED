About

AI Camp

AI Camp teaches future leaders about AI, coding, and how to work through creating impressive AI products and tech internships. During our 3 week of online camp we learned about NLP and concepts like transformers and backpropagation. Using the skills we learned throughout AI camp we were tasked with creating a final project which we call ToddEd.

About our project

We created an AI Model that generates Ted Talks. The purpose of this product so that people can efficiently get advice, help, and motivation with issues/topics that they are interested in.

Model

To build this product, we used GPT2Neo. GPT2 is an auto completion model, so our users can input a title or topic and as a result, the model will generate a Ted talk transcript the user is looking for.
